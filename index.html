Walkthrough

Step by step. No guesswork.

1

Rate Confirmation Arrives

The moment a rate confirmation hits your inbox, CarrierBliss reads it, extracts every detail, and logs the load automatically — no manual entry required.

2

Load Is Created

A new load record is generated instantly with all the relevant details — broker info, pickup and delivery locations, rate, and reference numbers — all in one place.

3

Driver Gets Assigned

Assign a driver to the load in seconds. They receive the full load details directly on their phone — no calls, no PDFs forwarded over text.

4

Driver Captures BOL

On delivery, the driver photographs the Bill of Lading directly in the app. It uploads instantly and appears in the admin load board — no chasing, no missing documents.

5

Load Delivered & Verified

The load status updates to delivered. All documentation is attached and verified. Your records are clean and ready for billing without lifting a finger.

6

Settlement Generated

Driver settlements and broker invoices are calculated automatically and exported as PDFs with one click. What used to take hours now takes seconds.
