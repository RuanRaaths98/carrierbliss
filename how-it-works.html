How It Works

From rate confirmation to driver settlement. Automated.

CarrierBliss was built by a carrier, for carriers. Every feature exists because a real dispatcher or owner-operator needed it. Here's exactly what happens inside the platform from the moment a load comes in to the moment a driver gets paid.

1

Rate Confirmation Arrives

The moment a broker sends you a rate confirmation PDF, CarrierBliss reads it using AI-powered data extraction. It automatically pulls the broker name, pickup and delivery locations, load reference numbers, commodity, rate, and any special instructions — and populates your load board instantly.

No more opening PDFs, copying data, and pasting it into a spreadsheet. No more fat-finger errors. The system does it in seconds, every time.

2

Load Is Created on the Board

A new load record appears on your load board with every detail pre-filled. You can sort, filter, and search across all active, pending, and completed loads in seconds — no scrolling through spreadsheet rows trying to find what you need.

Your entire operation is visible in one place. Every load. Every status. Every driver assignment. Updated in real time.

3

Driver Gets Dispatched

Assign a driver to the load with a single click. The moment you do, that driver receives the complete load details on their phone — pickup address, delivery address, broker contact, reference numbers, and any special instructions. No phone calls. No forwarding PDFs over text.

The Driver Portal is mobile-browser based, so there's nothing to install. Drivers just open a link and everything they need is right there.

4

BOL Captured on Delivery

When the driver arrives at the delivery location, they photograph the signed Bill of Lading directly in the Driver Portal. The image uploads instantly to the admin load board and gets attached to the load record automatically.

No more waiting for drivers to bring paperwork back. No more lost BOLs. Every document is stored, searchable, and accessible the moment it's needed for billing.

5

Broker Invoice Generated

With the load delivered and documents attached, generating a professional broker or shipper invoice takes one click. CarrierBliss pulls all the load data and creates a formatted PDF invoice ready to send — no manual calculations, no template editing.

Billing that used to take 20–30 minutes per load now takes seconds. At scale, that adds up to hours saved every single week.

6

Driver Settlement Calculated

Driver settlements are calculated automatically based on the load data and your per-mile or flat rate agreements. A PDF settlement statement is generated with one click — showing the driver exactly what they're owed, broken down clearly.

100% accurate. No disputes. No "let me check my spreadsheet." Drivers get paid on time and you maintain the paper trail to prove it.

7

Analytics Keep You Ahead

While your operation runs, CarrierBliss tracks the numbers that matter — Total Revenue, Average Rate per Mile, Load Profitability, and Driver Efficiency. The analytics dashboard gives you a clear, real-time view of how your fleet is performing so you can make better decisions faster.

No more guessing which lanes are profitable. No more end-of-month surprises. The data is always there, always current.
