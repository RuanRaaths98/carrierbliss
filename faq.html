FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Product & Features

How does the automated data extraction work? add

CarrierBliss uses AI to read your PDF rate confirmations. When you upload a confirmation, the system automatically populates the load tracking details, eliminating manual data entry.

What is the "Driver Portal"? add

It is a mobile-friendly interface where drivers can view their assigned load details, route information, and capture/upload Bills of Lading (BOL) directly from their phones upon delivery.

Does CarrierBliss handle invoicing? add

Yes. You can generate professional broker/shipper invoices and driver settlement statements with a single click based on the completed load data.

What kind of analytics does the platform provide? add

The dashboard tracks key performance indicators (KPIs) including Total Revenue, Average Rate per Mile (RPM), Load Profitability, and Driver Efficiency.

Technical & Setup

How long does it take to get started? add

Setup takes less than 5 minutes. You can create an account and begin managing loads immediately.

Do my drivers need to download an app? add

The Driver Portal is accessible via mobile browser, so drivers can view load details and upload BOLs without needing to manage a complex app installation.

Is my data secure? add

CarrierBliss is built with modern security standards in mind. See the Security link in the footer for specific protocols.

Pricing & Subscription

Is there a free trial? add

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial to get started.

How is CarrierBliss priced? add

We offer three tiers:

  • Starter ($49/mo) — Up to 10 trucks.
  • Pro ($99/mo) — Up to 30 trucks, includes team management and priority support.
  • Enterprise ($199/mo) — Unlimited trucks and custom integrations.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? add

Yes, you can change your plan at any time as your fleet grows or scales back.

Operations

How does CarrierBliss help with driver settlements? add

The system automatically calculates what is owed to the driver based on the load data and generates a PDF settlement statement, ensuring 100% accuracy and reducing disputes.

Can I manage dispatchers on this platform? add

Yes, the Pro and Enterprise plans include team management features and dispatcher settlement tracking.
